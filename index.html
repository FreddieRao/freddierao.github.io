RimdVAE: Rotation-invariant 3D Shape Retrieval Network.
Rao Fu, Jie Yang, JiaWei Sun, Lin Gao.
Submit to IEEE Transactions on Visualization & Computer Graphics (TVCG)
We introduce a network called RimdVAE, which is able to retrieve intra-class 3D shapes and extracts shape descriptor robust to rigid deformations. Through qualitative and quantitative experiments, we prove that rimdVAE is able to extract geometric details better and outperforms other state-of-the-art methods on intra-class global shape retrieval tasks.

Maya Workshop: LandRover Defender 90 Apr. 2020

Objection: Create a scence through 3D modelling, animation and visual effects
In this project, we use basic geometric shapes to modelling a indoor scence. We fine-tune to UV layout for texture mapping. Then, we use expressions to confine object motion. With different cameras and lightings, we made a shot movie of car driving scence.
